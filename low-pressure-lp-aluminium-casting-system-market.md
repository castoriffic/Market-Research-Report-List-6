<p><p>The Low Pressure (LP) Aluminium Casting System Market is primarily categorized into two types: Vertical and Horizontal Systems. Vertical systems involve the positioning of the mold in a vertical orientation, allowing for effective filling and minimized gas entrapment, making them suitable for complex shapes. In contrast, horizontal systems utilize a horizontal mold setup, which enhances throughput and is often preferred for mass production. Both systems cater to diverse industrial applications, delivering efficient casting solutions with varying advantages depending on production requirements.</p></p>
