<p><p>Refrigerated heating circulators are essential laboratory instruments used for precise temperature control in various applications. The market comprises two main types: ultra-low circulators and normal refrigerated heating circulators. Ultra-low circulators operate at temperatures significantly below ambient levels, often reaching -80°C, making them ideal for sensitive biochemical and cryogenic processes. Normal refrigerated heating circulators, on the other hand, offer a broader temperature range, typically between -10°C and 100°C, suitable for general heating and cooling tasks in various laboratory settings.</p></p>
