<p><p>Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) is a high-purity, non-hazardous solution composed of 32.5% urea and 67.5% deionized water, used in selective catalytic reduction (SCR) systems to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions in diesel engines. Compliance with stringent environmental regulations and the push for cleaner transportation solutions are driving the demand for DEF, as it ensures vehicles meet the required emissions standards.</p><p>The Diesel Exhaust Fluid Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to an increase in the adoption of diesel vehicles, particularly in commercial transportation, along with the expansion of the automotive sector in emerging economies. Technological advancements in SCR systems are also enhancing DEF efficiency, thus bolstering market demand.</p><p>Moreover, growing awareness of the environmental impact of diesel emissions and the rise in government initiatives for sustainable practices are contributing to market dynamics.
