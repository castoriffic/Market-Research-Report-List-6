<p><p>The semi-rigid drum liner market is categorized into various types, including PE (Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) drum liners. PE drum liners are known for their chemical resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for general storage. PP drum liners offer enhanced durability and heat resistance, suitable for harsher conditions. PVC drum liners provide excellent barrier properties but are less flexible. PVDC liners are renowned for their moisture and gas barrier capabilities, making them perfect for sensitive products.</p></p>
