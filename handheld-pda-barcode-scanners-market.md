<p><p>The handheld PDA barcode scanners market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired scanners connect directly to a computer or system via cables, offering reliable data transmission and consistent power. However, their mobility is limited due to the tethered connection. In contrast, wireless scanners utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology, allowing users greater flexibility and movement during scanning tasks. This enhanced mobility is advantageous in dynamic environments, although they may require battery management. Both types cater to varying operational needs.</p></p>
