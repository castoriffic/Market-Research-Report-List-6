<p><p>The Lithium Battery Weld Seam Inspection market encompasses two primary types: Module Inspection and Busbar Inspection. Module Inspection focuses on evaluating the weld seams within battery modules, ensuring structural integrity and performance reliability. This process is crucial for detecting defects that could lead to failures in energy storage. Busbar Inspection, on the other hand, examines the connections between battery cells and the busbars that transmit electricity, ensuring optimal conductivity and safety. Both inspections are vital for enhancing battery longevity and efficiency.</p></p>
