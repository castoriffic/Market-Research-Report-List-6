<p><p>Agriculture ventilation systems consist of various types designed to optimize air circulation in farming environments. Roof-mounted systems are installed on the top of buildings, allowing hot air to escape while promoting fresh air intake. Ridge ventilation, placed along the roof's peak, enhances airflow throughout the structure, effectively reducing humidity and maintaining temperature. Floor-mounted systems, positioned at ground level, facilitate air movement from the bottom up, ensuring even distribution and improving overall indoor climate for livestock or crops.</p></p>
