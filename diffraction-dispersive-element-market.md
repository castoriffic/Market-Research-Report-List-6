<p><ul><li>Ruled Dispersive Element</li><li>Holographic Dispersive Element</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diffraction dispersive element market primarily includes two types: ruled dispersive elements and holographic dispersive elements. Ruled dispersive elements are characterized by precisely spaced grooves etched onto a surface, which efficiently diffract light, making them suitable for various spectroscopic applications. Holographic dispersive elements, on the other hand, utilize interference patterns created by laser light to disperse light over a broader spectrum, offering higher efficiency and resolution. Both types cater to industries such as telecommunications, scientific research, and imaging technologies.</p></p>
