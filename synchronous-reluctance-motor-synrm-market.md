<p><p>A Synchronous Reluctance Motor (SynRM) is a type of electric motor that operates on the principle of magnetic reluctance, utilizing the magnetic reluctance of its rotor and stator. Unlike conventional motors, SynRMs do not require windings or permanent magnets in the rotor, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. This characteristic leads to higher efficiency levels and a robust design, making them suitable for various industrial applications, including fans, compressors, pumps, and conveyor systems.</p><p>The SynRM market is experiencing significant growth due to the rising demand for energy-efficient and sustainable motor solutions. Industries are increasingly adopting SynRMs as they provide excellent performance while reducing energy consumption and operational costs.
