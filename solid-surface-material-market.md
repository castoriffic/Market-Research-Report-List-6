<p><ul><li>Casting Molding Solid Surface</li><li>Extrusion Molding Solid Surface</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid Surface Material market is categorized into two primary types: Casting Molding and Extrusion Molding. Casting Molding Solid Surface involves pouring liquid acrylic or polyester resins into molds, allowing them to cure and harden, resulting in seamless designs ideal for countertops and sinks. Conversely, Extrusion Molding Solid Surface entails forcing heated material through a shaped die, creating continuous sheets that can be cut and shaped for various applications. Both types cater to diverse architectural and design needs, offering durability and aesthetic versatility.</p></p>
