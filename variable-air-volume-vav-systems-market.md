<p><ul><li>Single-Duct VAV</li><li>Dual-Duct VAV</li><li>Fan-Powered VAV</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) systems are categorized into several types based on their design and function. Single-Duct VAV systems control airflow to individual spaces using a single duct for supply air. Dual-Duct VAV systems utilize two ducts—one for hot air and one for cold air—allowing for more precise temperature control. Fan-Powered VAV systems incorporate a fan to boost airflow, enhancing performance and efficiency. Other VAV types may include dedicated outdoor air systems or variable refrigerant flow systems, catering to specific building needs.</p></p>
