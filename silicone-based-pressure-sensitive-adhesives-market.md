<p><ul><li>Peroxide Cure Pressure Sensitive Adhesives</li><li>Platinum Cure Pressure Sensitive Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone-based pressure-sensitive adhesives (PSAs) market includes two primary types: peroxide cure and platinum cure adhesives. Peroxide cure PSAs utilize organic peroxides to initiate the cross-linking process during curing, resulting in strong adhesion and thermal stability. In contrast, platinum cure PSAs employ platinum catalysts to facilitate curing, offering enhanced clarity, lower odor, and improved aging properties. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and healthcare for applications requiring durability and flexibility.</p></p>
