<p><p>Industrial automation systems encompass three primary market types: Fixed or Hard Automation, which is designed for high-volume production with specialized equipment; Programmable Automation, suitable for batch processes that require frequent changeovers and flexibility in production; and Flexible or Soft Automation, which allows for a high degree of adaptability and reconfiguration, accommodating varying product types and smaller production runs. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs, influencing efficiency, cost, and the ability to respond to market demands.</p></p>
