<p><ul><li>Roll Grooving</li><li>Cut Grooving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pipe Grooving Services market encompasses two main types: Roll Grooving and Cut Grooving. Roll Grooving involves the use of specialized machinery to create grooves around the pipe's circumference, allowing for efficient joining with fittings and couplings. This method is preferred for its speed and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Cut Grooving involves precisely cutting grooves into the pipe, offering greater control over dimensions and suitable for thicker materials. Both methods cater to diverse applications in plumbing, HVAC, and fire protection systems.</p></p>
