<p><strong>The Biocompatible Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEEK</li><li>PPSU</li><li>POM</li><li>PP</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biocompatible plastics market includes various types: PEEK (Polyetheretherketone), known for its durability and chemical resistance, is widely used in medical implants. PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone) offers high-temperature stability and is often used in surgical instruments. POM (Polyoxymethylene) provides excellent mechanical properties and is utilized in medical devices. PP (Polypropylene) is valued for its versatility and is commonly found in packaging and disposable items. Other biocompatible plastics include TPU and PLA, expanding applications across healthcare and packaging sectors.</p></p>
