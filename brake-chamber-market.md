<p><ul><li>Piston Type Brake Chamber</li><li>Diaphragm Type Brake Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake chamber market is primarily divided into two types: piston-type and diaphragm-type brake chambers. Piston-type brake chambers operate using a hydraulic mechanism, providing reliable braking force, especially in heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, diaphragm-type brake chambers utilize a flexible diaphragm to create braking pressure, allowing for more compact designs and easier installation. Both types cater to specific vehicle requirements, with diaphragm chambers often favored for their responsiveness and lighter weight, while piston chambers are preferred for their robust performance in demanding conditions.</p></p>
