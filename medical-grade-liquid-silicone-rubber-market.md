<p><ul><li>Limited Exposure</li><li>Prolonged Exposure</li><li>Permanent Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade liquid silicone rubber (LSR) market is categorized into three types based on the duration of contact with the human body: Limited Exposure, Prolonged Exposure, and Permanent Contact. Limited Exposure products are used temporarily for applications like disposable medical devices. Prolonged Exposure materials are suitable for devices that remain in contact for extended periods, such as catheters. Permanent Contact silicone rubber is designed for long-term implantation in the body, typically in surgical implants or prosthetics, ensuring biocompatibility and safety.</p></p>
