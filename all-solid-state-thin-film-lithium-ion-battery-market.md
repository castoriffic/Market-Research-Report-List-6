<p><p>The all-solid-state thin-film lithium-ion battery market is primarily categorized into two types: polymer electrolyte thin-film lithium batteries and inorganic solid electrolyte thin-film lithium batteries. Polymer electrolyte batteries utilize a polymer matrix to conduct lithium ions, offering flexibility and lightweight characteristics, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, inorganic solid electrolyte batteries employ rigid materials that enhance thermal stability and ionic conductivity, leading to improved safety and performance. Both types aim to advance energy storage technology while addressing safety and longevity concerns.</p></p>
