<p><p>The Building Automation Software market consists of two primary types: Building Management System (BMS) Software and Standalone Building Automation Software. BMS Software integrates various building systems, such as HVAC, lighting, and security, allowing streamlined management and enhanced energy efficiency. It provides centralized control and monitoring for larger facilities. Conversely, Standalone Building Automation Software focuses on specific functions, like HVAC control or lighting management, catering to smaller environments or individual systems without needing comprehensive integration. Both types aim to optimize building operations and reduce energy consumption.</p></p>
