<p><p>The pressure infusion set market comprises two main types: manual and automatic pressure infusion sets. Manual pressure infusion sets require healthcare professionals to manually inflate a pressure bag to maintain fluid delivery, offering flexibility and control. In contrast, automatic pressure infusion sets utilize built-in mechanisms to regulate pressure automatically, ensuring consistent fluid administration without manual intervention. Both types are essential in medical settings for delivering intravenous fluids efficiently, particularly in emergencies, but differ in their operational mechanisms and ease of use.</p></p>
