<p><p>The power electronics market is segmented into three main types: Power ICs, Power Modules, and Power Discretes. Power ICs integrate multiple components into a single chip, enhancing efficiency in compact designs. Power Modules combine various power components in a single package, offering higher performance and reliability for applications such as renewable energy and electric vehicles. Power Discretes are individual components like transistors and diodes, widely used for their versatility and ability to handle high voltages and currents in various applications.</p></p>
