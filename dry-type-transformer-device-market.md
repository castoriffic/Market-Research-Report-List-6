<p><strong>The Dry-Type Transformer Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Type</li><li>Shell Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry-type transformer market is segmented into two main types: core type and shell type. Core type transformers feature a simple design with the winding encapsulated around a magnetic core, providing efficient performance and easier maintenance. Shell type transformers, conversely, have a more complex structure, with the core enveloping the windings, enhancing protection and reducing electromagnetic interference. Both types are widely used in commercial and industrial applications due to their reliability, safety, and lower environmental impact compared to oil-filled transformers.</p></p>
