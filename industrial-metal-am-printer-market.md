<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF)</li><li>Material Extrusion (ME)</li><li>Directed Energy Deposition (DED)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial metal additive manufacturing (AM) printer market encompasses various types, including Powder Bed Fusion (PBF), Material Extrusion (ME), and Directed Energy Deposition (DED). PBF uses a laser or electron beam to fuse metal powder layers, offering high precision and complex geometries. ME extrudes metal-filled filament, benefiting from simplicity and cost-effectiveness. DED employs focused energy to melt materials as they are deposited, ideal for repairs and large-scale builds. Other emerging technologies also contribute to this diverse market landscape.</p></p>
