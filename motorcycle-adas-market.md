Anti-Lock Braking Systems (ABS) prevent wheel lock-up during braking, ensuring more controlled stops. Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) maintains a safe following distance by adjusting speed automatically. Other systems may include lane departure warnings, blind-spot detection, and automatic emergency braking, all aimed at improving rider safety and overall riding experience.</p></p>
