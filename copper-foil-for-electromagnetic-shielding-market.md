<p><p>The copper foil for electromagnetic shielding market comprises two main types: electrolytic copper foil and rolled copper foil. Electrolytic copper foil is produced through an electroplating process, offering excellent conductivity and thickness uniformity, making it ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, rolled copper foil is manufactured by mechanically rolling copper into thin sheets, providing durability and flexibility, suitable for various applications, including electronics, automotive, and telecommunications. Both types play crucial roles in providing effective shielding from electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
