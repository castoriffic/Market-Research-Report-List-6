<p><p>The COVID-19 vaccine market features various types, including component viral vaccines and whole virus vaccines. Component viral vaccines utilize specific parts of the virus, like proteins or genetic material, to stimulate an immune response without using the live virus, resulting in targeted protection. In contrast, whole virus vaccines use inactivated or attenuated forms of the virus, providing a broader immune response. Both types aim to prevent COVID-19 by training the immune system to recognize and combat the virus effectively.</p></p>
