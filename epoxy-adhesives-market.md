<p><p>The epoxy adhesives market is categorized into three main types: one-component, two-component, and others. One-component epoxy adhesives are pre-mixed and only require heat or curing for activation. Two-component epoxy adhesives consist of a resin and hardener that must be mixed before application, offering enhanced bonding strength and versatility. The "others" category includes specialty formulations designed for specific applications, such as flexible or low-viscosity adhesives. These variations cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs, ensuring effective bonding solutions across various sectors.</p></p>
