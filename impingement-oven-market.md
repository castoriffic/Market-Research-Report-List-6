<p><p>The impingement oven market is segmented into two main types: conveyor impingement ovens and countertop impingement ovens. Conveyor impingement ovens are designed for high-volume cooking, utilizing a continuous belt system to move products through the oven, ensuring uniform heating and efficiency. In contrast, countertop impingement ovens are compact and ideal for smaller spaces, catering to restaurants or catering services with limited kitchen area. Both types utilize forced hot air for rapid cooking, enhancing food quality and reducing preparation time.</p></p>
