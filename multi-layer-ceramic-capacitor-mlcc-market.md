High-frequency MLCCs are designed for applications like RF communication and high-speed data processing, where minimal inductance and capacitance variance is crucial. In contrast, low-frequency MLCCs cater to power supply filtering and decoupling, handling slower signal variations with higher capacitance values, making them ideal for general electronics and automotive applications.</p></p>
